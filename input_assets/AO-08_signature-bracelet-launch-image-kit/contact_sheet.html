Turn the brand's raw studio prototype shots, wordmark, and lifestyle frame into a launch image kit: a clean white-background e-commerce set, a color-graded close-up hero, an expanded social banner, and a vectorized wordmark — then assemble everything on a Firefly board for sign-off.

Full client brief
We are launching the signature bracelet for our new lifestyle and cultural brand (gathering, celebration, connection, memorable experiences). Prototypes are made and we have shot them in the studio, but the raw frames are crooked, dull, and shot on a slightly grey seamless that needs to read as pure white for e-commerce. We need you to produce the full launch image kit from the assets we supply — no new product design, just world-class post-production on what we shot.

Specifically:
1) Clean the hero studio shot (DSLR raw of the woven-cord-and-gold-clasp bracelet on a slightly-tilted grey seamless): auto-straighten the horizon, crop to a clean square, and bring it to life with auto-tone plus exposure/contrast and a slight warm white-balance so the gold clasp glows.
2) Make it e-commerce-ready: select the bracelet subject, invert the selection, and fill the background to pure white (#FFFFFF) so it drops cleanly onto our product pages; boost only the gold's saturation so the clasp reads as real 18k without touching the cord color.
3) From that cleaned, graded master, produce a punchy color-graded CLOSE-UP HERO for the homepage: apply a cohesive Lightroom look/preset and a gentle lens blur so the macro clasp detail pops, then crop to a 4:5 portrait.
4) Produce a wide SOCIAL/WEB BANNER from the same graded master by re-cropping the pure-white e-commerce master to a 16:9 landscape frame (2400x1350) — reframing so the bracelet sits to one side and the existing white sweep leaves clean text-safe negative space for headline text on the website hero. This is a crop/reframe of the existing pixels only; nothing is added or extended beyond the source frame.
5) We also supply a flat PNG of our wordmark — vectorize it to a clean SVG so it scales for packaging, the website, and the bracelet's hang-tag.
6) License one premium lifestyle "table of friends gathered" stock backdrop from Adobe Stock at full res so our social team has an on-brand scene to drop the banner over.
7) Assemble the final graded master, the white-background e-comm shot, the close-up hero, the expanded banner, the vectorized wordmark, and the licensed backdrop onto a single Firefly board so we can review and approve the whole kit in one place.

Deliverables: pure-white-background e-commerce JPEG, a 4:5 graded close-up hero, a 16:9 banner (cropped from the white-bg master) with text-safe negative space, a scalable SVG wordmark, the licensed stock backdrop, and a shareable Firefly board link. Keep the look elevated, warm, and timeless to match the brand.
