Full client brief
Hello -- I run a small family-history blog called Meadowlark Memory Lane and I have one very special photo I'd love professionally restored. It's a personal keepsake and means a lot to me: a color snapshot of my grandparents and my mother as a little girl, taken in a portrait studio in the mid-1970s. The physical print has aged badly and I've had it scanned at high resolution on a flatbed so you can work from the digital file.

The supplied scan (meadowlark_family_keepsake_1975.png) has these problems: the whole image has gone warm/orange and flat -- the color has faded and lost contrast; there are several fine white scratch lines and one short crease crossing the plain cream studio backdrop behind the family (they do NOT cross the faces); the scan sits very slightly crooked on the scanner bed with a thin ragged white border down one side; and overall the picture looks soft and washed out.

What I need: (1) straighten the slight scanner tilt and crop off the ragged white scan border so only the photograph remains; (2) correct the faded warm/orange color cast and recover natural, believable skin tones and clothing colors; (3) recover the faded contrast and lift overall clarity and local detail so the picture reads crisply on a framed print -- this is the "colors, details and sharpness" I care most about; (4) conceal the scratches and the crease, but ONLY where they cross the plain cream backdrop -- please colour-match that uniform area and fill the marks so they disappear (leave the faces and figures untouched); (5) add a very light film grain over the restored areas so the cleaned-up backdrop patch doesn't look plastic or smoothed against the original photographic texture; (6) deliver a clean, true-A4 file at 2480x3508 px / 300 DPI so I can send it straight to the print lab and frame it.

Deliverable: the single restored keepsake as a print-and-frame-ready A4 image (2480x3508 px, 300 DPI) I can send straight to the print lab and frame. Please keep it looking like a genuine 1970s photograph, just healthy again -- no modern, over-processed, plastic look.
