velvet_hour_raw_clips/ — a card-dump FOLDER of 12 raw, ungraded multi-cam concert clips (~24-33s each,
1920x1088 16:9, flat camera-original look, native-style camera audio muxed in) of a fictional solo
singer-songwriter playing a stripped-back acoustic set in a small dim warm-lit music room. Three angles:
locked wide, roaming handheld, close-up on guitar/hands. Blank unbranded gear (headstock has no wordmark),
no on-screen text, no music bed added in edit — the audio is the diegetic performance/tuning/room tone.
