Full client brief
We're launching DERMAVERA, a premium clinically-tested skincare line, and need a cohesive, credible visual identity executed across the product line (3 launch SKUs: a Vitamin-C Serum, a Barrier Repair Cream, and a Gentle Gel Cleanser). The look must be clean, minimalist, modern, science-driven and trustworthy — premium without being flashy, professional without looking pharmaceutical. We are supplying: (1) one raw hero photograph of the Serum bottle shot on a near-white sweep (slightly tilted, mixed lighting, cluttered edges); (2) a flat high-res raster of our chosen logo mark (a minimal molecule/droplet monogram + "DERMAVERA" wordmark) that we need delivered as a clean editable vector; (3) a soft lifestyle texture plate (matte stone surface) for collateral grounding; (4) a label-copy spreadsheet with one row per SKU (name, claim, volume, INCI line, batch placeholder, hex accent). We also supply our pre-authored print templates: an Illustrator label template (label.ai) with genuine Variables, and an InDesign unit-carton template (carton.indd). Deliverables: a flawless clinical white-background hero image (straightened, background swept to pure white, neutral clinical color, crisp product), a 1:1 e-commerce crop and a packaging-safe wide landscape reframe of the hero cropped to the target 5000×2800px (the pure-white-sweep master crop-and-resized to the wider ratio, centered on the product — sweep trimmed top/bottom, no canvas added), a clinical monochrome (single-accent duotone) brand variant of the hero, the logo delivered as an editable SVG, a recommended display+text type pairing, a print-ready per-SKU label set generated by merging our copy sheet into label.ai, a print-ready packaging PDF exported from carton.indd, and a single Firefly presentation board collecting the system for sign-off. Everything must be print-ready and leave us editable source (vector SVG + the authored .ai/.indd). Maintain consistent neutral, trustworthy color across every image.

**Template Description: label.ai**

This is a hand-authored Adobe Illustrator label template, 70x90mm with an additional 3mm bleed, set in CMYK color mode. The design features a static DERMAVERA vector logo positioned top-centre, a thin hairline rule, and a clean, clinical whitespace layout.

Dynamic content is driven by genuine Illustrator Variables (bound as Dynamic objects, not literal text), ensuring data merge compatibility. These variables include:
*   `{sku_name}` for the main display heading.
*   `{claim}` for a subhead.
*   `{volume}` placed in the lower-right corner.
*   `{inci_line}` as a small-print body block.
*   `{batch}` for footer micro-text.

A brand accent color is controlled by the `{accent_hex}` variable, which drives a specific swatch/text accent. All variable names (`sku_name`, `claim`, `volume`, `inci_line`, `batch`, `accent_hex`) directly correspond to expected CSV headers for `document_merge_data_vector` processing, enabling one label output per data row.
