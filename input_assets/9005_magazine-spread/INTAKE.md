- **Two Adobe Stock editorial textures — an uncoated paper grain and an ink-bleed wash — licensed at full print resolution to ground the body and ink-wash pages** → Sourced LIVE from Adobe Stock at execution via asset_search (entityScope StockAsset, contentType photo/texture) then asset_license_and_download_stock for full-res print downloads (license-before-edit): (1) an uncoated paper-grain texture searched as 'uncoated paper grain texture editorial' -> graded with image_adjust_light_portions into the subtle GROUND_PAPER page ground; (2) an ink-bleed/ink-wash texture searched as 'ink bleed wash texture monochrome' -> tinted with image_apply_color_overlay toward the duotone shadow ink #1A2A33 into the GROUND_INK ground.
