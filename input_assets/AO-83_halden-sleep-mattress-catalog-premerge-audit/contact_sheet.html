Full client brief
Halden Sleep is a boxed-mattress startup about to send its 2026 wholesale catalog to the printer and to its retail-partner intake team. We already have (a) an authored InDesign catalog template (halden_catalog_template.indd) — a 2-up A4 landscape product-spread layout with real InDesign data-merge fields (<<sku>>, <<model>>, <<size>>, <<firmness>>, <<material>>, <<height_in>>, <<weight_lb>>, <<cert>>, <<box_dims>>, <<price>>, plus an <<image>> frame per product) and (b) a product roster CSV (mattress_roster.csv) with one row per SKU that our ops team filled in by hand. Before we spend money on a print run and before the retail intake team rejects us, we need someone to VALIDATE the roster against the template and against the marketplace-style mandatory-spec checklist — the same fields a catalogue approval team checks: size, material, firmness level, certification, weight, and packaging (box) dimensions must be present and non-blank on EVERY SKU, prices must be formatted consistently, firmness must be one of a controlled vocabulary (Soft / Medium / Medium-Firm / Firm), and specs must be consistent across all SKUs (no unit drift, e.g. some rows in cm and some in inches). Deliverable: a plain-English validation findings report (halden_catalog_audit.md/pdf) that lists, per SKU, exactly which mandatory field is missing, mis-formatted, off-vocabulary, or inconsistent, with a prioritized fix-list ranked by blocker vs. warning; plus a proof: run a data-merge of the FIRST sample spread through the authored template and export it so we can eyeball whether any merge frame comes out blank or text oversets. Do NOT redesign the catalog — the .indd layout is final; we only need the data checked and a proof spread rendered. Page size A4 landscape, 300 DPI proof. Please also confirm every referenced product image resolves (no missing/placeholder frames) by previewing the sample.

**Layers:** The document is organized into standard layers for clarity and control:
*   `Background & Guides`: Non-printing guides, subtle background elements.
*   `Images`: Contains all `<<image>>` frames.
*   `Text & Data`: Houses all text frames including data merge fields and static copy.
*   `Branding & Logos`: Dedicated for the "Halden" brand logo and any recurring graphic elements.

content
1.  **Mandatory Fields:** The following fields are required and must not be blank for any product entry: `size`, `material`, `firmness`, `cert`, `weight_lb`, `box_dims`.

2.  **Firmness Controlled Vocabulary:** The `firmness` field must use one of these exact values: `Soft`, `Medium`, `Medium-Firm`, `Firm`. No other values are permitted.

3.  **Unit Consistency:**
    *   All linear dimensions (e.g., product dimensions, `box_dims`) must be specified in `INCHES`.
    *   The `weight_lb` field must be specified in `POUNDS`.
    *   Units `cm` or `kg` are not accepted.

4.  **Price Formatting:** The `price` field must be consistently formatted as `$#,###.##` (e.g., `$1,234.56`, `$999.00`).

5.  **SKU Uniqueness:** Each product `sku` must be unique across the entire catalogue submission.

6.  **Image Resolution:** All image URLs referenced in the `images` field must be valid and resolve to an accessible image file.
