Full client brief
Ember & Oak is a fictional wood-fired neighbourhood grill, and we need a short-form social reel that makes people hungry the second it starts. This is for Instagram Reels, TikTok and Facebook. We want it fast, warm and mouth-watering — think tight cuts, live fire, steam, a cheese pull, a chocolate pour — cut to a punchy music track, with our menu items and prices flashing up and our logo landing at the end. Lean budget, quick turnaround.

Source material we're supplying: (1) four raw 16:9 clips straight off the camera — a wood-fired ribeye being sliced with steam rising, live flames on the grill as the chef lays steaks down, a fork twirling truffle rigatoni with a sauce/cheese pull, and warm chocolate poured over a molten dessert; every clip is flat and slightly under-exposed / cool straight out of camera and needs to be warmed up to look appetising; (2) one plated hero still of the ribeye on our dark oak table, also flat and slightly tilted, to become the cover/thumbnail; (3) our logo as a flat single-colour PNG; (4) a quick voiceover our owner recorded on his phone in the middle of the busy dining room — one warm line, but there's clatter and chatter all over it, so it needs cleaning before it's usable; (5) a menu-callout CSV with the four dishes, their prices and a one-line descriptor; and (6) a brand-kit / reel brief with our colours, font feel, tagline, the on-screen hook line, the CTA and hashtags.
