Full client brief
Foxglove Records, a small independent label that co-runs a volunteer community-radio arts programme, is booking a run of on-air ad spots to promote its spring 'Porch Sessions' live-music series. This is an assembly-and-clean job, not a from-scratch creative project — everything is already recorded and the label is handing it all over. Supplied: (1) a :60 voiceover read of the full spot recorded by a volunteer host on a USB mic in a spare room (warm, conversational, but with steady room hiss, a bit of HVAC hum, and light room reverb); (2) a tighter :30 cut-down read of the same script; (3) a spoken station/legal tag line ('Foxglove Community Radio, 98.3 — supported by listeners like you') recorded in the same room; (4) a supplied instrumental music bed (a gentle acoustic guitar loop the label already cleared for on-air use) as a WAV; and (5) a rough 'guide mix' the host quickly recorded on a phone with the voiceover talking over the music bed, so you can hear the intended pacing. There is also a script + timing cue sheet (CSV) listing each line, its target in/out seconds, and which take it belongs to.

What the label needs back is a clean, organised, mix-ready audio package for the two spot lengths: every spoken recording denoised and de-reverbed down to an isolated, broadcast-clear speech stem so the voice sits clean; the supplied music bed carried through untouched as the bed; the guide mix listened back and summarised so the confirmed script/timing cue sheet matches what was actually read; and all of it gathered into one dated Creative Cloud handoff folder with the cue sheet, so the label's volunteer engineer can drop the stems onto the timeline and do the final layer-mix. IMPORTANT and stated up front: the final multi-track mix itself — ducking the voiceover over the music bed, loudness/broadcast mastering to the station's target level, and the cross-fades — is a manual finish in the engineer's DAW and is out of scope for this automated audio-prep pass; this pass delivers the cleaned speech stems, the confirmed cue sheet, the carried-through bed, and the organised package. Deliverables: cleaned :60 VO speech stem, cleaned :30 VO speech stem, cleaned station-tag speech stem, the confirmed script/timing cue sheet, the supplied music bed carried into the package, and one organised mix-ready Creative Cloud handoff folder.
