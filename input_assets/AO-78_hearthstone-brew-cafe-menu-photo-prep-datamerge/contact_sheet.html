Full client brief
Hearthstone Brew Co., a warm neighborhood café, needs its full food-and-drinks menu finished for print. The owner has handed off an authored InDesign menu template (hearthstone_menu_template.indd) laid out in a warm, cozy, elevated style — clean type, generous margins — with five photo frames and real data-merge fields (<<section>>, <<item>>, <<desc>>, <<price>>) arranged under four section headers (Breakfast, All-Day Plates, Pastries, Coffee & Tea). Alongside it come the brand wordmark as a flat PNG, five raw phone-shot photos (a flat white, a stack of buttermilk pancakes, an avocado toast plate, a butter croissant, a cortado in a glass), and a CSV holding every menu row (section, item name, one-line description, price). The photos are unappetizing as-shot: slightly tilted, dull and flat, shot under cool café-window light with busy table clutter behind them. The job: (1) make every food/drink photo look warm and appetizing — straighten, auto-tone, warm the white balance toward a cozy morning-light look, lift the shadows, push vibrance, apply a warm food-pop preset to lock a consistent look across all five, and throw the cluttered café background out of focus so each dish reads as hero; consistently crop all five to the 4:5 frame ratio the template's photo frames expect; (2) lift the flat-white hero cup cleanly onto a transparent background so it can sit on the kraft texture without a hard photographic edge; (3) vectorize the flat wordmark PNG into a clean SVG so it stays razor-sharp at large menu-board size; (4) license a warm kraft-paper / linen texture from Adobe Stock to back the layout and reinforce the cozy feel; (5) upload all the prepped assets, then run the client's authored .indd through an InDesign data-merge against the menu CSV so each section / item / description / price binds into the template and the prepped photos + wordmark + texture sit in their frames; (6) export the bound document to a print-ready A4-portrait PDF (~300 DPI) and a high-resolution JPEG for the café's social and delivery-app listings; (7) assemble the graded photos, vector wordmark, and final menu render onto a Firefly board so the owner can approve the photo treatment and finished menu from one deep-link. Deliver print-ready files plus the graded photos and vector wordmark as the editable source assets.

**Template File:** `hearthstone_menu_template.indd`

**Description:**
This is an Adobe InDesign document (.indd) designed as a single-page, A4 portrait menu template for Hearthstone Brew Co. The template embodies a warm, cozy, and elevated aesthetic, utilizing clean serif and humanist typography with generous margins for a refined, non-template-heavy presentation.

**Dimensions:** A4 portrait (210mm x 297mm).

**Structure & Layers:**
*   **Background:** A full-bleed image frame is positioned at the bottom layer, designated for a kraft-paper texture.
*   **Branding:** A dedicated placeholder area at the top of the page is reserved for the "Hearthstone Brew Co." wordmark.
*   **Imagery:** Five distinct image frames, each set to a 4:5 aspect ratio, are strategically placed throughout the layout for visual menu item highlights.
*   **Section Headers:** Pre-formatted text frames delineate four primary menu sections: "Breakfast," "All-Day Plates," "Pastries," and "Coffee & Tea."

**Placeholders & Data Merge:**
The template is configured with genuine Adobe InDesign data-merge placeholder fields, ensuring direct binding for dynamic content population. These fields are arranged under the respective section headers:
*   `<<section>>` (for category or sub-section titles)
*   `<<item>>` (for menu item names)
*   `<<desc>>` (for item descriptions)
*   `<<price>>` (for item pricing)
