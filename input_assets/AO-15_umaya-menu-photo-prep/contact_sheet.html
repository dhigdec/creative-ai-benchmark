Full client brief
Umaya is a ramen + sushi restaurant refreshing its existing tri-fold dine-in menu (22.5"x12" unfolded, print-ready CMYK 300 DPI). They are adding several new ramen bowls and an entirely new Wagyu section, and the brand look is fixed: dark background, hero food photography, red accent elements, Japanese ramen/sushi aesthetic. The page layout itself is being hand-typeset by their designer in the existing Adobe Illustrator source; this engagement is the PHOTOGRAPHY + BRAND-ASSET production that feeds that layout.
