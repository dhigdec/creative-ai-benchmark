Full client brief
Pulsevault is a downtown nightclub, and we run a themed event every Friday. We need a punchy 30-second recap of last weekend to post on Instagram Reels and TikTok to promote the next one — high-energy, fast-paced, and capturing the vibrant atmosphere of the room. We already have all the raw material; we just need it cut together and finished properly. Here is what we're handing off: (a) three raw vertical clips shot on a phone from the DJ booth and the floor (crowd hands-up, laser/haze sweep, and a confetti drop), all a bit dark and mixed-lit with the club's colored wash; (b) one hero still grabbed from the footage that we want turned into the cover/thumbnail frame; (c) our venue wordmark logo as a flat PNG; (d) a rough spoken hype line recorded on a phone in the office (room noise + reverb) that we want cleaned up to lay under the intro; and (e) an event-info sheet (next date, doors, ticket link, hashtag) as data for the on-screen captions. Deliverables: 1x final ~30-second 9:16 MP4 ready to post on Instagram and TikTok (Full HD); a matching cleaned voiceover track; an on-screen captions/event-info asset; and an exported, fully color-graded 1080x1920 cover frame with our logo locked up in the corner as a crisp vector so it stays sharp. Keep the look on-brand: deep club darkness, punchy neon magenta/cyan pop, energetic. Source assets should stay in our Creative Cloud folder so tweaks are easy each week. NOTE up front: frame-accurate beat-synced cuts, hard transitions, animated motion graphics, and music syncing are a manual Premiere/After Effects finish and are out of scope for the automated connector pass — the automated pass delivers the AI sizzle assembly, the 9:16 reframe, the VO cleanup, the captions asset, and the graded cover frame; our editor does the final timeline polish on top.
