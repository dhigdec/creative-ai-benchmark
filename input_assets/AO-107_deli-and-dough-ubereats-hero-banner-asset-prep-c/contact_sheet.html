Full client brief
Deli & Dough is a scrappy New-York-style sandwich shop in Western Sydney — gritty, bold, a little chaotic, think NY deli meets Nonna's kitchen meets street culture, not polished or corporate. We're setting up our Uber Eats storefront and need our hero banner finished. We already have the assets; they just need to be pulled together so they look like they were shot in one image — one cohesive, intentional photo, NOT a collage. Here is the hand-off: (1) an authored Adobe Illustrator banner template (banner_template.ai) built to EXACTLY 2880px wide x 2304px high (the Uber Eats hero spec), containing named placement frames — a full-bleed background frame, a hero-sandwich frame, three supporting food frames, and a wordmark frame — plus a small data-merge slug row so the tagline/handle text binds in; (2) four raw phone-shot deli photos — the hero pastrami-on-rye stacked high, a supporting Italian sub, a supporting basket of house pickles, and a supporting drip-coffee cup; (3) our wordmark as a flat cream-on-red PNG; (4) direction to back everything on a gritty butcher-paper / deli-counter texture. The four photos are inconsistent as shot: different white balance (some cool, some warm), tilted, dull and flat, busy backgrounds. The job: (a) grade all four to ONE consistent gritty-warm deli look — straighten, auto-tone, warm the white balance, lift the shadows/contrast, push vibrance a touch, and lock the look with a single shared preset so they truly read as the same shoot; crop each to the frame ratios the template expects; (b) lift the hero pastrami sandwich cleanly onto a transparent background so it drops onto the butcher-paper backdrop without a hard rectangular photo edge (that hard edge is what makes it look like a collage); (c) source and license a rustic butcher-paper/deli-counter backdrop texture from Adobe Stock; (d) vectorize the flat wordmark PNG into a clean SVG so it stays razor-sharp at banner size; (e) upload the prepped parts and run our authored .ai template through a data-merge so each graded photo, the cutout hero, the vector wordmark, the backdrop, and the tagline slug bind into their named frames; (f) export the bound banner to a print-ready PNG and a max-quality JPEG at EXACTLY 2880x2304. Keep it gritty and warm; do not make it clean-corporate.

**Template File Description:**

**File Name:** `banner_template.ai`

**Purpose:** Uber Eats hero banner, designed for high-impact promotional campaigns across digital platforms.

**Dimensions:** 2880px (width) x 2304px (height).

**Structure & Layers:** The template is meticulously laid out with specific, named placement frames and data-merge text fields to enable efficient content population:

*   **`<<bg_texture>>`**: A full-bleed background frame, covering the entire canvas, intended for a texture or background image.
*   **`<<hero_cutout>>`**: A prominent, large frame positioned on the left side, designed to showcase a transparent hero food item (e.g., a sandwich cutout).
*   **`<<support_1>>`**, **`<<support_2>>`**, **`<<support_3>>`**: Three distinct supporting frames, typically arranged to the right or below the hero, for graded sub-photos of additional menu items or promotional imagery.
*   **`<<wordmark>>`**: A dedicated frame for the Uber Eats wordmark or a campaign-specific logo, typically placed in a corner.
*   **Data-Merge Text Row**: A pre-formatted text slug row configured for data-merge functionality, containing:
    *   **`<<tagline>>`**: A field for the primary promotional message or offer.
    *   **`<<handle>>`**: A field for a secondary identifier, call-to-action, or social media handle.

**Brand Styling:** The template adheres to a clean, modern, and food-centric aesthetic consistent with Uber Eats branding. It prioritizes clear visual hierarchy, vibrant food imagery, and legible typography to maximize promotional impact.

**Functionality:** This template is explicitly authored for real data-merge binding, ensuring that all named placement frames and text fields are correctly linked for automated content integration from an external data source.
