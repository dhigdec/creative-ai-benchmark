Full client brief
We run a custom football/soccer kit shop and need our signature jersey numbering font rebuilt as proper vector artwork for DTF, Flex and Flock production. We are sending two reference images shot off one of our printed jerseys: one sheet showing the full uppercase alphabet A–Z, and a second sheet showing the digits 0–9. The phone shots are slightly tilted, unevenly lit, and have a faint fabric texture and color cast from the jersey weave, so they need cleanup before tracing. We also include a licensed close-up of the jersey collar so you can match the exact ink tone. Deliverables: clean vector recreations of the complete glyph set with consistent stroke widths and even spacing, exported as SVG + EPS + AI for the letter set AND separately for the number set. The vector paths must be tight enough to cut on a Flex/Flock plotter and to separate cleanly for DTF. We hold commercial rights to the original printed artwork; the recreation must be original vector geometry, not extracted from any proprietary font file. Please process the letters and the numbers as two parallel passes off the same cleanup recipe so the two sheets stay visually identical in weight and proportion, and hand back a single organized package.

Why it's non-trivial
Long-horizon and wide-mix: 24 steps, 14 distinct Adobe tools across 3 connector groups (image_* raster cleanup, document_* vector export, asset_* stock/upload/storage). It demands real planning — a shared deterministic cleanup recipe applied identically across two parallel branches for cross-set consistency, correct ordering (straighten before tone before select, license before edit), and honest handling of the connector's limits: vectorize is the only path to the SVG, fill_area is used strictly as a solid two-tone flattener (not generative), and the .ai/EPS masters are EXPORTED via document_render_vector from authored vectors rather than composed from scratch. Difficulty comes from glyph-edge fidelity (crushing fabric weave without eroding stroke weight) and keeping the letter and number sets a coherent family, not from any single hard step.
