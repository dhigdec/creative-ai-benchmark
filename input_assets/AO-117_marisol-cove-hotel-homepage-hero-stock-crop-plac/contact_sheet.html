Full client brief
Marisol Cove is a fictional boutique clifftop hotel on the Amalfi-style coast, refreshing its homepage. We need a premium, luxury-style homepage hero banner in two sizes — a Desktop hero (1920x720 px, wide cinematic band) and a Mobile hero (1080x1350 px, tall portrait) — that share one consistent image and brand identity. We do NOT have our own hero photography yet, so please license a suitable high-resolution widescreen coastal shot from Adobe Stock (think: turquoise sea, terraced clifftop villas, warm late-afternoon light, generous empty sky on one side so our headline and logo have room to breathe) — it must be large enough to crop both the wide desktop band and the tall mobile frame without any upscaling. Grade the licensed image to our warm "golden-hour coastal" look: lift the exposure a touch, warm the white balance, push vibrance so the sea and terracotta pop, and keep it clean and premium (no heavy filters). For both crops leave clear open space on the left third for our headline "Escape to the Cove" and our logo lockup — do not let the buildings or horizon crowd that zone. We are handing you our exact wordmark as a flat PNG (cream "Marisol Cove" with a small wave mark) — please preserve it exactly, vectorize it to a crisp SVG, and place it top-left in both banners; do not redraw or restyle it. We are also handing you our authored InDesign banner template (marisol_hero_template.indd) that already contains the desktop and mobile artboards with a background image frame, the logo frame, and a <<headline>> / <<tagline>> data field pair, plus a one-row CSV with our copy — please run the merge so the copy and placed assets bind, then export: a web JPEG of the desktop hero, a web PNG of the mobile hero, and a print-ready PDF proof of both artboards for our sign-off deck. Finally, drop the two graded crops, the vectorized logo, and the final banners onto a review board so our founder can approve in one link. Brand colors are deep teal (#0E5A62), terracotta (#C4652E) and cream (#F4EBDD).

**Template File:** `marisol_hero_template.indd`

**Description:**
This is an Adobe InDesign document containing two artboards for the Marisol Cove homepage hero section:

1.  **Desktop Artboard:** 1920x720 px
2.  **Mobile Artboard:** 1080x1350 px

**Structure & Layers (per artboard):**
Each artboard features a layered composition designed for responsiveness and brand consistency:
*   **Background Image Frame:** A full-bleed image frame positioned behind all other elements, intended for a "graded coastal crop" visual.
*   **Logo Frame:** Anchored to the top-left corner of the artboard.
*   **Text Area:** Dynamically populated via real InDesign data-merge fields.

**Placeholders & Data-Merge Fields:**
*   `<<headline>>`: Placeholder for the primary hero text.
*   `<<tagline>>`: Placeholder for secondary hero text.
*   **Background Image:** Image frame for the coastal visual.
*   **Logo:** Image frame for the brand logo.

**Brand Styling & Layout:**
The design ensures a consistent brand presence and clear messaging:
*   The left third of both the Desktop and Mobile artboards is intentionally kept clear of the background image's focal subject.
*   This designated clear area is reserved for the `<<headline>>` and `<<tagline>>` text, along with the logo, forming a distinct "headline/logo lockup."
