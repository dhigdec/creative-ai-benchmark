# Aura & Clay: Brand Guidelines

## Brand Story

Aura & Clay celebrates the gentle art of handcrafted ceramics, each piece a whisper of earth's beauty and human touch. We craft homeware that brings a quiet sense of calm and authentic warmth into your sacred spaces. Our creations invite you to slow down, appreciate the artisan's journey, and find beauty in the everyday.

## Palette

| Name          | Hex       | Usage                                      |
| :------------ | :-------- | :----------------------------------------- |
| Clay White    | `#F8F4EE` | Primary background, clean text, minimalist spaces. |
| Desert Rose   | `#C27664` | Warm accents, highlights, key calls to action. |
| Sage Whisper  | `#7A8E7A` | Secondary accents, organic elements, grounding tones. |

## Typography

*   **Headings:** Lora (Serif)
*   **Body Text:** Inter (Sans-serif)
*   **Social Media Sizes:**
    *   Headline: Lora, 24-32px
    *   Subhead: Inter, 18-22px
    *   Body/Caption: Inter, 14-16px

## Logo Usage Rules

*   **Clearspace:** Maintain a minimum clear space around the logo, equal to the height of the 'A' in Aura, on all sides.
*   **Don'ts:**
    *   Do not stretch or distort the logo.
    *   Do not alter the colors.
    *   Do not place the logo on busy backgrounds that obscure visibility.
    *   Do not add additional elements or effects to the logo.

## Photo Style

*   Warm, diffused natural light.
*   Emphasis on artisan ceramic textures and tactile surfaces.
*   Linen fabric accents in neutral tones.
*   Subtle inclusion of dried botanicals.
*   Sun-drenched, minimalist interior settings.
*   Soft focus to evoke a gentle, dreamy feel.

## Tone of Voice

Gentle and inviting, like a comforting whisper. We aim to evoke a sense of calm, authenticity, and artisan appreciation through thoughtful, understated language. Our voice is serene, respectful, and always mindful of the quiet beauty in everyday moments.

### Example Captions

1.  "Each curve tells a story, each glaze holds a whisper of the earth. Bring a piece of quiet artistry into your morning ritual."
2.  "Creating spaces that feel like a gentle embrace. Our handcrafted vessels are designed to ground your home in serene beauty."
3.  "Slow living begins with mindful choices. Discover the authentic charm of ceramics crafted with intention and a loving touch."
