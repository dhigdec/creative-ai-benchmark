Full client brief
We sell handcrafted sterling silver religious jewelry and have just finalized a new MODERN-style rosary necklace (clean geometric links, minimal crucifix, brushed-silver finish). I shot it on my bench under mixed lighting and need a freelancer to build the full commercial product package we can use across our store listing, lookbook, a stamped/printed merch concept, and our wholesale line sheet.

Deliverables:
1. A retouched HERO image of the rosary: straightened, auto-toned, exposure/highlight balanced so the brushed silver reads true (not yellow-cast), the subject isolated, dropped onto a clean neutral studio sweep, cropped and resized to the exact 4:5 e-commerce frame, and given a final commercial polish (a subtle vibrance/saturation lift so the silver pops) — the flat neutral sweep already reads as a soft, distraction-free studio background. Deliver a polished JPEG and the cut-out transparent PNG.
2. A clean VECTOR line-art / silhouette of the necklace (SVG) we can scale for stamping, embroidery, and the modern logo-lockup spec sheet.
3. A screen-print / halftone TECH GRAPHIC of the rosary (single-tone, print-screen look) for a tote/tee merch mockup and our stamping vendor.
4. A print-ready PRODUCT SPEC SHEET: take our authored InDesign spec-sheet template and our SKU CSV (material, chain length, crucifix size, finish, price tiers) and data-merge it into a per-variant PDF, plus a vector variant of the line sheet from our Illustrator template.
5. Assemble the hero, the cut-out, the vector spec, the halftone graphic and the spec-sheet preview into a single Firefly review board (deep-link) so we can run revision rounds.

Keep everything modern, minimal, and silver-true. I will pick the final crop. Strong communication for revisions is essential.
