Full client brief
I have raw footage for an informative explainer video that needs to be shaped into a clear, engaging cut, plus the supporting brand assets to dress it. Source material I'm supplying: (1) a bundle of raw clips — a ~3-minute talking-head segment intercut with product/process b-roll; (2) a separate scratch voiceover WAV recorded in a noisy room that needs cleaning before it can carry the edit; (3) my company logo as a flat PNG; (4) a single "color reference" photograph that represents the warm, slightly cinematic brand look I want the whole piece graded toward; and (5) a loose narrative outline.

I need the freelancer to handle the full edit and the supporting graphics, mapped to Adobe Creative Cloud connector tools, with every step honestly tagged for execution mode:

A. AUDIO — clean my scratch voiceover so it can drive the cut: denoise / de-room the voice track (voice-only; this is not music mastering).

B. VIDEO — (1) summarize the raw footage so we agree on the key talking points and a logical flow before cutting; (2) produce a punchy ~60-second highlight/sizzle cut from the raw clips that surfaces those key points; (3) reframe that cut to a 9:16 vertical version for social teasers, same length (no trimming).

C. BRAND COLOR LOOK — from my color-reference still, build the locked "brand LUT" look the editor will match the master grade to: straighten and crop it clean, then run the full Lightroom-class grade (auto-tone baseline, exposure, highlights, dark-portion lift, warm color-temperature push, HSL + vibrance/saturation shaping, a gentle preset and fine grain) so I have one authoritative graded reference frame.

D. GRAPHIC PLATES — the on-brand overlays the editor lays onto the timeline (delivered as standalone transparent PNG / SVG plates, NOT composited onto the video): (1) vectorize my logo to a clean SVG so it scales for the end-card and corner bug; (2) build a lower-third bar plate — solid brand-color bar generated by selecting a region and filling it; (3) build a call-out badge plate the same way; (4) grade the end-card hero frame to match the brand look.

E. PACKAGING — gather the graded reference, the plates, the cleaned VO, and the video deliverables onto a Firefly board so the whole linked-asset set is organised for easy future tweaks, exactly as I want the Premiere project handed off.

Deliverables on approval: cleaned voiceover; a ~60s 1080p H.264 sizzle cut and a 9:16 reframe (same length); one authoritative graded brand-reference frame; logo SVG; lower-third and call-out PNG plates; a graded end-card frame; and a Firefly board linking the whole organised asset set. Two rounds of feedback should be plenty.

Why it's non-trivial
Long-horizon flagship: 25 connector calls, 21 distinct Adobe tools, spanning four groups (asset/storage, video/audio [A], photo/raster grade [C], vector + board). It exercises an 11-link linear color-grade dependency chain (each adjust_* consuming the previous frame), a video sub-chain where a summary prompts a sizzle cut that is then reframed, a selection→solid-fill plate sub-chain reused twice, and a cross-branch match (end-card grade references the locked look frame).
