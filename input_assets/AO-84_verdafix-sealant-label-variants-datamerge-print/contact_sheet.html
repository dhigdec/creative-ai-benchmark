Full client brief
Verdafix Industrie GmbH, a European maker of construction sealants and adhesives, is converting its product-label artwork from legacy flat PDFs into a maintainable, print-ready system. The team has already rebuilt ONE master label into an authored InDesign template (verdafix_label_master.indd) - a 90 x 130 mm bottle/canister wrap laid out with 3 mm bleed, carrying real InDesign data-merge fields (<<product_name>>, <<variant>>, <<net_content>>, <<sku>>, <<batch_prefix>>, <<h_statements>>, <<p_statements>>, <<usage_de>>) plus image frames for the product shot, the GHS hazard pictogram, and the EAN barcode. What they need now is production, not design: bind the master to the full product range and output one press-ready file per SKU/variant. Supplied assets: a variants CSV (verdafix_label_variants.csv, one row per SKU/variant across the MS-Polymer, Hybrid and Acryl ranges in 290 ml and 600 ml sizes - 12 rows total) whose column headers exactly match the merge fields and which references each variant's product-photo and barcode filenames; a folder of raw product photos shot on the line (slightly tilted, dull, cool-cast studio table shots that need leveling, tone and a consistent crop to the template's photo frame); pre-rendered EAN-13 barcode PNGs and the GHS02/GHS05/GHS07 hazard-pictogram PNGs that must be vectorized so they stay crisp at press resolution. Brand + compliance constraints: labels are CMYK with 3 mm bleed at 300 DPI; German label copy and the H/P hazard statements come only from the CSV and must not be paraphrased; the hazard pictograms and barcodes must be sharp vectors, not soft raster; the recreated look must match the existing labels (no new design). Deliverable: the graded photo set, vectorized hazard/barcode graphics, and a set of print-ready CMYK PDFs - one per SKU/variant - merged from the master template, ready to hand to the label printer.

This is a genuine Adobe InDesign master template for a Verdafix sealant-bottle wrap label, meticulously rebuilt to match the brand's existing legacy aesthetic and styling.
