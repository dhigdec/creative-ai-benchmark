Full client brief
I run a small packaging design studio and I'm launching a new website. I have a folder of finished work — six photographs/mockups of packaging projects I've shipped (a tea carton, a cosmetics jar, a coffee bag, a craft-beer can, a perfume box, and a chocolate bar sleeve), all shot in my studio over the past year so they don't match: some are warm, some are cool, a couple are slightly crooked, exposure drifts shot to shot. I also have one clean photo of a plain kraft box I want to use as the title card — please seat it on a clean solid studio card so I have even negative space to overlay my logo.

I want all of this pulled together into an eye-catching HERO sizzle reel for the top of my homepage: fast-paced, energetic, and making my design work look its absolute best. Please do the following, in order, so the look is cohesive across every shot:

1) Bring all my supplied photos in and get them straight and uniformly exposed (auto-straighten the crooked ones, auto-tone, lift the dark portions a touch).
2) Give the whole set one consistent grade — neutralize the mixed white balance toward a clean studio look, push vibrance so the print colors pop, apply a single cinematic Lightroom preset across every frame, and add a light film grain so they read as one family.
3) For the title card: seat the kraft box on a solid charcoal studio card so there's clean negative space for my logo, and also make a clean "cut-out" hero variant of the cosmetics jar — remove its background and drop it onto a solid charcoal card.
4) Crop/resize every graded frame to a uniform 1920x1080 so they sequence cleanly.
5) Pull in one licensed studio-texture stock plate to use as the intro/outro background.
6) Assemble the graded frames into a fast, energetic ~20-second sizzle reel at 1920x1080 for the website hero, and also give me a 1080x1080 square cut for Instagram.

Quality bar: every shot must look like it belongs to the same set; print colors must be punchy but not blown out; the title-card plate must be clean with even negative space.
