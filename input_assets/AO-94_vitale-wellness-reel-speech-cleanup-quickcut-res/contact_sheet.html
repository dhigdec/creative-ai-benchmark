Full client brief
Vitalé Labs is a direct-to-consumer wellness and DNA-supplement brand that publishes short educational clips explaining how personalized nutrition works. We just filmed our lead nutrition scientist, Dr. Nadia Reuter, doing a single continuous take (about 3.5 minutes, shot 3840x2160 landscape on a mirrorless camera) explaining our new "GenoBalance" DNA supplement panel. It is a rough take: she rambles, restarts a couple of sentences, there are long pauses while she checks her notes, and the room has an audible HVAC hum plus a little echo because we shot in an untreated office. We need this turned into ONE tight, premium, trustworthy sizzle Reel — clean and modern and educational, NOT flashy or meme-style. Deliverables and specs: (1) clean up the spoken audio so her voice is clear and the room hum/echo is gone; (2) cut the rambling take down to a punchy ~35-45 second highlight version that keeps the strongest, most watchable moments and drops the dead air — keep viewers glued from the first second; (3) from that finished cut, export three platform-ready versions at the correct dimensions with no stretching: a 9:16 vertical Instagram Reel (1080x1920), a 9:16 vertical YouTube Short (1080x1920), and a 1:1 square feed post (1080x1080); (4) also hand back a plain-text caption/subtitle-and-hook note (transcript of the take plus a 3-second hook line and hashtag set) that our team will drop onto the timeline ourselves for burned-in captions. Keep the brand feel restrained and professional per our kit. Attached: the raw take, a one-page paper-edit / hook script marking the lines we care about, and our brand kit.
