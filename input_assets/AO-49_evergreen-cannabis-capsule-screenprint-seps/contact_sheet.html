Full client brief
Evergreen (a CT/OH/MO cannabis retailer) is producing a Fall/Spring 2027 elevated-streetwear capsule sold in-store and via loyalty redemption, and needs the hero front-print graphic handed off to a screen-print decorator in production-ready form. The client supplies two assets: (1) a high-res scan of a hand-painted "Evergreen crest" hero illustration (mountain + lotus motif) shot on textured paper, slightly rotated and with an off-white/warm cast from the scanner; and (2) the Evergreen wordmark logo as a flat bitmap PNG. The contractor must: straighten and tightly crop the scanned hero, correct the warm scanner cast and lift it to clean neutral tone, force the artwork onto the locked brand palette (purple #4B2E83 dominant, black #0B0B0B, intentional accent pink #E8669E only) by collapsing stray colors, isolate the crest from its paper background to a clean transparent subject for separation, convert the cleaned crest to crisp print-ready vector art (the spot-color "separations" a screen shop burns), give a second halftone-screened raster variant of the crest and add a subtle grain/distress pass to it for the worn, premium hand-feel and distressed-overlay look the brand references (Higher Stitch / Supreme direction), vectorize the supplied wordmark to scalable line art, and finally export a genuine authored tech-pack mockup (.ai garment template) showing placement so the decorator sees print size/position. Everything is bundled into one handoff package for the manufacturer. Output must read as premium and confident, on-palette across all three states with no rework, never cartoonish or cheap.
