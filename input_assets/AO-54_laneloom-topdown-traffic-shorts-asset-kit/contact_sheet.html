Full client brief
LaneLoom is a modern driving-education brand (beginner, nervous, teen, and defensive drivers) producing mobile-first vertical shorts (1080x1920) of clean, top-down animated traffic explainers — lane changes, turns, parallel parking, highway merging, 4-way stops, hazard recognition. We are standing up a 10-template content system and need the reusable, brand-consistent SOURCE-ASSET PACKAGE that our After Effects animator will build the templates from. (We are NOT asking the connector to animate — keyframing stays with our AE artist. We need the upstream art kit + downstream delivery prep.)

Supplied by us: (1) a flat-rendered top-down reference frame of a 4-lane road junction (PNG, on a neutral asphalt background); (2) a separate "icon plate" PNG holding our reusable top-down car body, turn-arrow, and yield/stop-sign glyphs laid out on white; (3) our LaneLoom wordmark/logo PNG (transparent); (4) a scratch voice-over WAV (a presenter explaining a 4-way-stop, recorded on a noisy laptop mic); (5) one rough ~12s After Effects preview render (16:9, 1920x1080) of a lane-change move that we want test-reframed to the vertical short canvas.

We need, as one chained job:
- A clean, scalable SVG icon set (cars, arrows, signs) extracted from the icon plate so the AE artist can drop infinitely-scalable vectors into every template — this is the heart of the "reusable" requirement.
- ONE locked brand color grade (cool, high-clarity, slightly desaturated asphalt, punchy lane-line yellow) applied identically to the reference junction frame AND the cover frame so all 10 templates read as one brand.
- A 9:16 vertical COVER / END-CARD frame: reframe (crop) the graded junction frame to the 9:16 short aspect so the road sits centered with clean headroom, then seat a solid LaneLoom-brand charcoal title band into the upper region for the title and the logo lockup, with the wordmark color-matched in.
- A licensed, subtly-grained seamless asphalt texture (from stock) the animator can tile as the road base.
- The scratch VO denoised to broadcast-clean voice.
- The rough AE preview reframed to 1080x1920 (same length) so we can sanity-check the vertical crop before committing the template.
- Everything assembled onto a single shareable Firefly board as the kit hand-off, and a font recommendation for the on-road label/caption type.

  9. image_select_by_prompt — Select the top headroom region of the vertical crop to receive a solid brand title band.

  14. media_enhance_speech — Denoise the noisy scratch VO to broadcast-clean voice (speech-only enhance). Async-widget. DELIVERABLE (clean VO).
  15. video_resize — Reframe the 1920x1080 AE preview to 1080x1920 vertical, SAME length (no trim/convert). Async-widget. DELIVERABLE (vertical preview).
