I'm a mortgage lender working Solano County and the greater Bay Area, positioning myself as the local financing expert. I need the Adobe production pieces that go into my "Open House Finance Flyer" marketing package (front and back, print + digital). Style must read sophisticated, luxury-but-approachable, clean and modern, in black/white with brass/gold accents that appeal to Bay Area homebuyers.

Assets I'm giving you: a hi-res exterior listing photo of the open-house property (shot slightly tilted, mixed afternoon light), my professional headshot, my brand wordmark as a flat PNG, my CLIENT-AUTHORED InDesign flyer template (front/back, with image frames + a tagged payment-scenario table carrying real <<merge fields>>), and a CSV of monthly-payment / down-payment / appreciation scenarios for several price points.

What I need you to do:
1. Straighten and clean up the property exterior, fix the tilt and the mixed-light exposure, and crop it to the hero frame.
2. Give the property shot a consistent luxury grade — warm brass/gold-leaning, rich blacks — and make a banner-ratio version by cropping and resizing the graded hero to the wide hero-band ratio so it fills the wide hero band on the flyer without cropping the house.
3. Isolate the house from its background so I have a clean cut-out version for the digital/social variant.
4. Retouch and color-match my headshot to the same luxury look so it sits next to the property shot.
5. Vectorize my wordmark to a clean SVG so it stays crisp at any print size and on the QR-code/contact strip.
6. Export the finished front/back flyer from my authored InDesign template, and run the InDesign data-merge so each price scenario in my CSV produces its own print-ready page (purchase price, down-payment options, monthly payment, appreciation projection).
Deliver the graded hero photo (print + banner), the cut-out, the matched headshot, the SVG wordmark, the exported flyer PDF, and the merged multi-page scenario PDF, plus a Firefly board collecting the final approved assets for my sign-off.

**Template Description: `open_house_finance_flyer.indd`**

This is a 2-page (front/back) InDesign template for an 'Open House Finance Flyer', formatted to US Letter dimensions (8.5" x 11").

**Front Page Structure:**
*   **Hero Image Frame:** A wide, top-aligned image placeholder (`HeroImage_Frame`).
*   **Design Element:** A decorative brass/gold horizontal rule below the hero image.
*   **Headline Area:** A prominent text frame for a main headline (`Headline_Text`).
*   **Branding:** A dedicated image frame for a company logo (`CompanyLogo_Frame`).
*   **Lender Profile:** An image frame for a lender's headshot (`LenderHeadshot_Frame`).

**Back Page Structure:**
*   **Payment Scenario Table:** A pre-formatted table designed to display financial scenarios, containing the following live InDesign data-merge fields:
    *   `<<PurchasePrice>>`
    *   `<<DownPaymentOptions>>`
    *   `<<MonthlyPayment>>`
    *   `<<AppreciationProjection>>`
*   **Property Information:** A text frame containing the data-merge field `<<PropertyAddress>>`.
*   **Contact Information:** A bottom strip featuring a QR code placeholder (`QRCode_Frame`) and contact details.

**Styling & Features:**
*   **Color Palette:** Incorporates brass/gold accents, suggesting a sophisticated and financial brand aesthetic.
*   **Placeholders:** All text and image frames are named for easy identification and content population.
*   **Data Merge Ready:** Utilizes genuine InDesign `<<field>>` placeholders for direct data binding via data merge.
