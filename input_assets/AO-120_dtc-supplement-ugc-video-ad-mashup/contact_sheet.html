NOURA is a direct-to-consumer wellness brand — a daily greens + adaptogen powder that (in our words) 'actually tastes like something you'd drink on purpose.' We run paid social on TikTok, Instagram Reels, Meta feed and YouTube, and we're scaling our UGC ad program. For this assignment we're handing over a batch of raw creator footage and want it cut into a short-form ad package we can put straight into ad sets and A/B test.

NOURA Daily Greens - Brand Kit Notes

**Brand Colors:**
*   **Fresh Green:** #3FA45C (Primary)
*   **Warm Cream:** #F5EEDD (Background/Text)
*   **Deep Forest Accent:** #1E3A2B (Secondary/Text)
*   **Sunny Highlight:** #F4C63D (Accent)

**Typography Intent:**
*   **Headlines & Captions:** A friendly, rounded geometric sans-serif typeface (e.g., Gotham Rounded, Montserrat) to convey approachability and modernity.
*   **Body Text:** A clean, legible humanist sans-serif (e.g., Open Sans, Lato) for clear, comfortable reading across all platforms.

**Brand Voice:**
Our voice is **warm, honest, and a little cheeky**, always aiming to be relatable and encouraging. We are **never clinical or overly serious**, preferring a lighthearted yet informative tone that empowers our audience without overwhelming them.

**Usage Notes:**
*   **Lower-Third Captions:** Utilize a Fresh Green (#3FA45C) bar with Warm Cream (#F5EEDD) text for clear, consistent on-screen information.
*   **Fact Callouts:** Present key facts within a Warm Cream (#F5EEDD) badge with Deep Forest Accent (#1E3A2B) text for emphasis and readability.

**Compliance:**
All ad copy uses structure/function language only — no disease, cure, treatment, or medical claims, per DSHEA-style supplement advertising rules.
