Scale My Meal Prep has delegated this week's creative package for the client "Fuel'd Kitchen", who is launching a High-Protein Chicken Bowl. The client uploaded four raw assets: (1) a hand-held studio hero photograph of the plated chicken bowl (slightly tilted, mixed lighting, flat color, shot wider than the banner crop), (2) ~70 seconds of raw 4K horizontal product-showcase b-roll of the bowl being assembled and steaming, (3) a phone-recorded founder voiceover ("Real food, prepped for real life…") with room hiss and HVAC hum, and (4) a flat full-color PNG of the Fuel'd Kitchen wordmark logo for the meal-prep container labels.

- A screen-print-ready vectorized (SVG) version of the wordmark logo for the branded meal-prep container labels.
- A Firefly Board deep-link assembling the finished package for client review.

Work fast and to spec — the agency measures this role on turnaround. Use only Adobe Creative Cloud connector tools; every editing step must build on the previous one.
