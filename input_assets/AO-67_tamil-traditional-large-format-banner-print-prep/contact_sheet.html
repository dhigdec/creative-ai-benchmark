Why it's non-trivial
Long-horizon (28 connector calls) across three Adobe groups (image_*, document_*, asset_*) with 16 distinct Adobe tools — well past the >=10 calls / >=6 distinct-tools bar. It is a true dependency chain: an 8-step hero grade that must be applied in the correct order (straighten before tone, highlights before warming, mask before masked-saturation, bleed last), a branched logo-vectorize path, an authored-master export path that renders all six masters individually, a PDF->.indd back-conversion, and a final packaging fan-in that consumes every deliverable. All print-spec acceptance criteria (CMYK PDF, 150 dpi at full scale, 1-inch bleed, untouched Tamil type) are honoured by the export/authoring split.
