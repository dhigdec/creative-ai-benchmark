# Lesson 4 — How Compound Interest Works

## 1. BRAND KIT

**Brand Name:** Meridian Academy

**Color Palette:**
*   Deep Indigo: `#23255A` (Primary for text, backgrounds)
*   Warm Gold: `#F2B441` (Accent for highlights, key elements)
*   Soft Cream: `#F7F4EC` (Backgrounds, secondary text)

**Typefaces:**
*   **Titles/Headers:** Friendly display serif (e.g., Fraunces, Playfair Display)
*   **Body/Lower-Third:** Clean sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Open Sans)

**Tone:** Warm, encouraging, approachable, plain-English, empowering. Avoid jargon where possible, or explain it clearly when necessary.

## 2. LESSON SCRIPT OUTLINE

**Core Beats:**
1.  **Hook (0:00-0:30):** Introduce the magical idea: "Your money can earn money on money." Set the stage for financial growth.
2.  **Definition & Mechanism (0:30-1:45):** Explain compound interest simply – interest earning interest. Contrast with simple interest briefly.
3.  **The Growth Curve Visual (1:45-3:30):** Illustrate how growth accelerates over time, especially with consistent contributions. Emphasize patience and time.
4.  **The Rule of 72 (3:30-4:45):** Introduce this quick calculation to estimate doubling time. Give a practical example.
5.  **Call to Action: Start Early (4:45-5:30):** Reinforce the power of time and the importance of starting to invest early, even with small amounts.
