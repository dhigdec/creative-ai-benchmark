- Step 12 tool choice is questionable: it uses image_fill_area to 'place the finished hero on a solid pure-white 5000x2800px field.' image_fill_area is a solid-color region fill on an existing raster, not a canvas-extension/compositing op. Going from a ~4000x5000 portrait hero to a WIDER 5000x2800 landscape white field requires enlarging/re-canvasing the image and positioning the product on it — that is padding/reframe work better expressed via image_crop_and_resize (fit-onto-white) than fill_area. As written the step does not obviously produce the stated wider-than-source canvas without either generative expand (correctly banned) or a compositing step the connector does not own. Recommend switching step 12 to image_crop_and_resize (pad-to-white to 5000x2800) or clarifying how fill_area yields a larger landscape canvas from a portrait source.
