The template utilizes genuine InDesign image frames for all visual content (1 large hero, 3 small detail). **Dimensions:**
Optimized for standard print dimensions (e.g., A4 or US Letter equivalent), ensuring balanced margins and clear presentation.

**Brand Styling:**
The design embodies a luxury aesthetic, characterized by clean lines, precise alignment, and a sophisticated typographic hierarchy.
