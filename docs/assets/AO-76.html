**Template Description: `auto_rate_card.indd`**

This is a single-page A4 (210mm x 297mm) portrait Adobe InDesign document, serving as a Northlantic Insurance Personal Auto rate card. It is fully brand-locked to Northlantic's style guide.

**Brand Styling:**
*   **Colors:** Northlantic Slate (#1F2A44), Signal Amber (#F4A82C).
*   **Typography:** Headlines use Basis Grotesque, body text uses Source Serif.

**Structure & Layers:**
1.  **Background Layer:** Contains a full-bleed graphic frame intended for a textured background image.
2.  **Header Layer:** Includes a dedicated placement frame for the Northlantic Insurance logo at the top.
3.  **Content Layer:**
    *   Features four distinct, styled coverage-band headers: "Liability Only", "Standard", "Standard Plus", and "Full Coverage".
    *   Under each header, content is structured with repeating rows utilizing genuine Adobe InDesign data-merge placeholder fields.
    *   **Data-Merge Fields:**
        *   `<<coverage_tier>>`
        *   `<<coverage_desc>>`
        *   `<<annual_premium>>`
        *   `<<monthly_premium>>`
        *   `<<deductible>>`
    *   These fields are meticulously arranged to bind correctly with external data sources for dynamic content generation.
4.  **Footer Layer:** A clearly defined, boxed frame at the bottom contains placeholder text for legal disclosures and general footer information.

This template is an authentically authored InDesign file, ensuring the data-merge fields are live and functional for direct binding, rather than being flattened text.

**Art Direction Note: Background Texture for Rate Tables**

For all rate tables across our Northlantic materials, we require a very subtle off-white premium paper / linen texture as the background. This texture must be low contrast and nothing loud; its primary function is to add a touch of sophistication without ever reducing the legibility of the overlaid text. Please think along the lines of high-quality cream stationery with a faint fiber grain—absolutely no distracting pattern motifs or overt designs. The goal is a clean, understated elegance that enhances, not competes with, the content. When searching for options on Adobe Stock, please use terms like: 'subtle cream linen paper texture', 'off-white fine paper grain'.
