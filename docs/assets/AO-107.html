**Template File Description:**

**File Name:** `banner_template.ai`

**Purpose:** Uber Eats hero banner, designed for high-impact promotional campaigns across digital platforms.

**Dimensions:** 2880px (width) x 2304px (height).

**Structure & Layers:** The template is meticulously laid out with specific, named placement frames and data-merge text fields to enable efficient content population:

*   **`<<bg_texture>>`**: A full-bleed background frame, covering the entire canvas, intended for a texture or background image.
*   **`<<hero_cutout>>`**: A prominent, large frame positioned on the left side, designed to showcase a transparent hero food item (e.g., a sandwich cutout).
*   **`<<support_1>>`**, **`<<support_2>>`**, **`<<support_3>>`**: Three distinct supporting frames, typically arranged to the right or below the hero, for graded sub-photos of additional menu items or promotional imagery.
*   **`<<wordmark>>`**: A dedicated frame for the Uber Eats wordmark or a campaign-specific logo, typically placed in a corner.
*   **Data-Merge Text Row**: A pre-formatted text slug row configured for data-merge functionality, containing:
    *   **`<<tagline>>`**: A field for the primary promotional message or offer.
    *   **`<<handle>>`**: A field for a secondary identifier, call-to-action, or social media handle.

**Brand Styling:** The template adheres to a clean, modern, and food-centric aesthetic consistent with Uber Eats branding. It prioritizes clear visual hierarchy, vibrant food imagery, and legible typography to maximize promotional impact.

**Functionality:** This template is explicitly authored for real data-merge binding, ensuring that all named placement frames and text fields are correctly linked for automated content integration from an external data source.
