Fizzwild Brand Kit Notes

**Brand Name:** Fizzwild
**Product:** Craft Kombucha

**Brand Voice:** Bold, warm, energetic. Our tone should feel adventurous and inviting, reflecting the wild and vibrant flavors of our kombucha.

**Primary Color Palette (Hex Codes):**
*   **Brand Teal:** #0E8C7F (Our core brand color, representing natural refreshment)
*   **Sunburst Amber:** #F5A623 (Accent color, evoking warmth, energy, and flavor)
*   **Off-white Cream:** #FAF3E0 (Neutral background, providing a clean canvas)

**Website Asset Slots & Pixel Dimensions (W x H):**
Please ensure all assets are provided at these exact dimensions.

*   **Desktop Hero Image:** 1920x1080px
*   **Mobile Hero Image:** 1080x1920px
*   **"Our Flavors" Section Header Image:** 1600x600px
*   **Call to Action (CTA) Card Image:** 1080x1350px
*   **Promotional Banner Image:** 1456x180px

**Important Note on Photography & Overlays:**
All photographic assets submitted for the above slots *must* be clean images, free of any baked-in text or logos. Our development team will be responsible for overlaying headlines, CTA buttons, and the Fizzwild wordmark live on the website. This allows for dynamic content updates and optimal responsiveness across devices.
