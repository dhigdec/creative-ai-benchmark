Umbra Loft - Brand Ink Specifications

For noir duotone posters and promotional imagery, please use the following brand inks:

Shadow Ink: deep ink-blue #10131F (RGB 16,19,31)
Highlight Ink: warm amber #E9A23B (RGB 233,162,59)

Mapping Instructions: The performer should read as amber highlights emerging from deep ink-blue shadows; keep it dark and moody, edges falling to near-black.

Consistency Note: All three photos must be treated identically for a matched series.
