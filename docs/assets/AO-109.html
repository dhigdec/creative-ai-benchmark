KANTYNA NOVA: BRAND PHOTO DIRECTION

**Client:** Marta Zawadzka
**Brand:** Kantyna Nova (Modern Polish Comfort Food & Catering)

**OVERALL AESTHETIC GOAL:**
To visually represent Kantyna Nova as a warm, inviting, and contemporary Polish comfort food experience. Images should evoke a sense of deliciousness, authenticity, and approachable quality, moving away from dated or 'canteen-like' perceptions. We aim for an elevated yet natural feel.

**PHOTOGRAPHIC DIRECTIVES:**

*   **White Balance & Color:** Crucially, correct any cool, drab 'canteen-green' cast often associated with institutional kitchens. Aim for a distinctly warm and rich white balance. Colors should feel inviting and true to life, enhancing the natural appeal of the food and environment.
*   **Shadows & Light:** Shadows should be gently lifted, ensuring detail and warmth are retained without flattening the image. Avoid harsh, deep shadows. Light should feel natural and soft, suggestive of a welcoming space rather than a studio setup.
*   **Vibrance & Saturation:** Elevate vibrance to make food look exceptionally appetizing and fresh. Colors should pop just enough to be enticing, but without appearing oversaturated or artificial.
*   **Contrast:** Apply gentle contrast. We want depth and visual interest, but avoid overly dramatic or harsh contrast that can make food look uninviting or processed.
*   **Processing & Grade:** The overall look must be natural and authentic, not over-processed or overly stylized. A consistent grade across all images is paramount to ensure brand cohesion. Each image should feel like part of a unified visual story for Kantyna Nova.

**TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS:**

*   **Desktop Hero Crops:** All primary hero images for desktop use should be delivered at a 16:9 aspect ratio, specifically 1920x1080 pixels.
*   **Mobile Crops:** All primary images for mobile use should be delivered at a 4:5 aspect ratio, specifically 1080x1350 pixels.
