Pre-press Guidelines: Ironclad Elevator & Electrical Supply

This note outlines critical brand and pre-press specifications for all Ironclad Elevator & Electrical Supply print assets. Our official brand colors are Industrial Navy (#1B2A41) and Safety-Orange Accent (#E8621F). Please utilize `Ironclad_Logo_Primary_CMYK.ai` for our primary logo, ensuring it is always used in its original vector format. All print layouts should be designed for US Letter portrait (8.5" x 11") dimensions.

For all product listings, catalogs, and data sheets, the following section order is mandatory: Contactors & Relays; Elevator Door Operators; Push-Button Fixtures; Fuses & Breakers; and Cables & Connectors. This sequence must be strictly adhered to across all materials.

Final print proofs will be audited against the following non-negotiable acceptance criteria: no part number may be clipped, and no voltage/amperage/price cell may be blank on the printed card. Any proof failing these specific checks will be rejected and require immediate correction.

**Template Description: `parts_catalog_template.indd`**

**File Type:** Adobe InDesign Document (.indd)

**Purpose:** This template is designed as a US Letter portrait B2B parts catalog, optimized for data merge to generate individual part specification cards.

**Dimensions:** US Letter (8.5" x 11"), portrait orientation.

**Structure & Layout:**
The template is structured to display multiple "spec cards" per page, with each card detailing a single part. Content is logically grouped under five primary section headers:
1.  Contactors & Relays
2.  Elevator Door Operators
3.  Push-Button Fixtures
4.  Fuses & Breakers
5.  Cables & Connectors

A consistent running header and footer are present across all pages. Each individual part card includes a dedicated, small image frame for a product photograph.

**Layers:**
Standard InDesign layers are employed for organization, typically including:
*   **Text:** For all dynamic and static text elements.
*   **Images:** For product photo frames and any static graphics.
*   **Background/Graphics:** For layout elements, rules, and background colors.
*   **Headers/Footers:** For recurring page elements.

**Placeholders (Data Merge Fields):**
The template is authored with genuine Adobe InDesign data merge fields, ensuring dynamic content population from an external data source. These fields include:
*   `<<part_no>>`
*   `<<description>>`
*   `<<voltage>>`
*   `<<amperage>>`
*   `<<list_price>>`
*   `<<section>>` (used for section grouping/navigation)

**Brand Styling:**
The design follows a clean, professional, and highly functional B2B aesthetic. Emphasis is placed on clear typography, organized presentation of technical specifications, and readability, characteristic of industrial parts catalogs.

**Critical Audit Finding (Intentional Authoring Defect):**
The text frame specifically designated for the `<<part_no>>` data merge field has been deliberately authored with a narrow width. Crucially, auto-fit options (such as "Auto-Fit Frame to Content" or similar) are *not* enabled for this frame. This intentional design flaw will cause text overset and clipping for any alphanumeric part codes that exceed the frame's limited width when data is merged or the document is exported, providing a genuine finding for content overflow during the audit process.
