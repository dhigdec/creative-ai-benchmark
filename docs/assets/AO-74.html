**File:** `crumbco_catalog_template.indd` (Adobe InDesign Document)

**Purpose:** Multi-page wholesale product catalog for Crumb & Co. bakery, designed for data merge automation.

**Dimensions & Orientation:** US Letter (8.5" x 11"), portrait orientation. Document includes a 0.125" bleed and standard half-inch margins.

**Structure & Placeholders:**
*   **Cover Page:** Features a full-bleed panel for hero imagery, with a dedicated masthead text frame positioned for the "Crumb & Co." wordmark.
*   **Master Pages:** Configured for repeating product-cell grids (typically 2x3 or 3x4 cells per spread), ensuring consistent layout.
*   **Product Cell (Repeating Unit):** Each cell contains:
    *   **Image Frame:** A 1:1 (square) aspect ratio frame, mapped for a data merge image column (e.g., `@Product_Image.jpg`).
    *   **Data Merge Text Fields:**
        *   `<<product_name>>`: Prominent, bold heading for the product title.
        *   `<<sku>>`: Smaller text, typically italicized, for the stock keeping unit.
        *   `<<description>>`: Body text frame for product details.
        *   `<<case_pack>>`: Concise text frame for packaging information.
        *   `<<wholesale_price>>`: Large, bold text frame for pricing.
*   **Section Headers:** Dedicated, styled text frames for content organization: "Breads", "Pastries", "Cakes & Slices", and "Cookies & Bars", strategically placed on master pages or as overrideable elements.
*   **Layers:** Organized into logical groups (e.g., "Background Graphics", "Images", "Product Text", "Master Page Elements") for ease of editing.

**Brand Styling (Crumb & Co.):**
*   **Typography:** Employs a clean, legible sans-serif for body copy and data fields (e.g., Montserrat or Lato), complemented by a slightly more decorative serif or script for section headers and the masthead, conveying a crafted, artisanal feel.
*   **Color Palette:** Predominantly warm, earthy tones (creams, light browns, charcoal) with a subtle accent color (e.g., a muted berry or sage green) to reflect a natural, inviting bakery aesthetic.
*   **Overall:** Professional, organized layout with ample white space, prioritizing clear product presentation and ease of readability.

**Technical Notes:** All specified `<<data_merge_fields>>` are correctly defined and bound within the InDesign Data Merge panel, ready for dynamic content population from a CSV/TXT data source, including the image frame mapping.
