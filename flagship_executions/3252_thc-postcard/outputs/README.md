## Copy & acceptance checks (verified programmatically + visually)

- All copy rendered **verbatim from `postcard_copy.json`** (curly quotes/apostrophes intact; no retyped strings). The only non-JSON visual element is the drawn copper arrow after each product-section name (Futura has no U+2192 glyph) — a layout connective, not copy.
- "Legal in Missouri" is the single amber-filled, bold, larger pill among the five key points.
- All 9 brand marks placed in the JSON `brands` order, connector-cut, legible at print size (cells ~1.39"x0.45" at 300dpi).
- Both QR codes are the real supplied codes: front 480px (NEAREST integer-ish upscale of the 444px original), back placed **bit-identical at native 444px** (verified). Quiet zones ≥4 modules of light ground (24px white internal border + ≥40px cream/white panel padding).
- Compliance line (21+ / hemp-derived / 2018 Farm Bill / Missouri law) runs as fine print along the bottom of the back, inside the trim.

## Honest limitations

- **PDF/X-4 prepress conversion** (ICC profiles, CMYK separation) is a print-house step; this PDF is RGB @300dpi with bleed + trim crop marks.
- **Editable Adobe Express source**: not generated by this pipeline (see table) — flagged for human handoff.
- The compliance fine print sits 89px from the artwork edge (inside the trim, slightly inside the usual 0.25" safe zone) by design, as the brief asks for fine print "along the bottom edge".
- Photography is used at its supplied resolution (1536px sources cover-cropped; no upscaling claimed as print-res).
