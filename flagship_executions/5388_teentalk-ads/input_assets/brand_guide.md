# TeenTalk Retargeting Statics: Brand Guide

This guide ensures our retargeting statics resonate genuinely with exhausted parents. Our tone is emotionally intelligent, serious-warm, and credible. We avoid anything cheesy, clickbait, or clinical, focusing on empathetic understanding and practical solutions.

---

## 1. Palette

| Colour        | Hex       | Role                            |
| :------------ | :-------- | :------------------------------ |
| Soft Teal     | `#3AA8A0` | Calm background fields          |
| Purple        | `#6B4FA1` | Brand colour, wordmark + stamp  |
| Warm Gold     | `#F2B23E` | CTA chips ONLY                  |
| Deep Ink      | `#232A31` | Headline text                   |
| Warm Off-White | `#FAF6EF` | Light fields                    |

**Note:** Teal and purple carry the emotion. Gold is reserved for the single action moment.

---

## 2. Stamp Usage

The round purple TeenTalk stamp (`teentalk_stamp.png`) sits **SMALL** in the top corner of every creative (roughly 7-9% of canvas width). Ensure clearspace of at least half the stamp's diameter on all sides. Never enlarge into a hero logo, never recolour, never place over a face.

---

## 3. Mobile-First Type Hierarchy

For all three formats (9:16 Stories/Reels, 4:5 Feed, 1:1 Square), elements appear in this reading order:

1.  **Emotional visual first**
2.  **Headline** (Libre Franklin)
3.  **ONE short supporting line** (Lora)
4.  **CTA block**
5.  **Small TeenTalk stamp**

The headline must survive a phone screen at feed size, ensuring immediate readability.

---

## 4. Do / Don't

**Do:**
*   Real, candid mother/teen imagery.
*   Serious, warm, credible tone.
*   Generous negative space.
*   Main text readable on mobile.

**Don't:**
*   Overly staged "perfect family" stock.
*   Childish design.
*   Generic therapy-clinic style.
*   Aggressive clickbait.
*   Overloading the canvas with text.

---

## 5. CTA Chip

Use a warm gold `#F2B23E` rounded chip with deep ink label text. Keep it concise: 2-4 calm words. Implement exactly one chip per creative.
