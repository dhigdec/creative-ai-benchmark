## Brief
Biotech - a manufacturer with 32 years in rehabilitation equipment - is launching RADIVA, a next-generation Solid-State Radial Pressure Wave Therapy (RPWT) system, and needs a premium ~8-page product brochure that positions it against leading medical-device brands (Zimmer, Storz Medical, BTL, Chattanooga). This is explicitly NOT a generic product catalog: the brochure must communicate Innovation, Intelligence, Clinical Confidence and Premium Technology, with a clean, modern, minimal, technology-driven European medical-device aesthetic. RADIVA's selling points must be surfaced as structured spec/feature content: Solid-State Electromagnetic Technology, Soft Shock Technology (patient comfort), the BIOS (Biotech Operating System) running an RTOS on a quad-core 64-bit ARM processor, a 10-inch capacitive touchscreen, a built-in clinical protocol library with user-created and QuickLink protocols, and real-time parameter adjustment during treatment.\n\nThe brochure has a fixed 8-page running order: (1) Cover, (2) RPWT Overview, (3) Solid-State Technology, (4) Soft Shock Technology, (5) BIOS Software Platform, (6) Intelligent Clinical Workflow, (7) Real-Time Treatment Control, (8) Closing / Company Experience. The client PROVIDES finished product photography (a clean studio device hero), software screenshots, the company logo, brand rules and detailed content; the freelancer sources any supporting clinical/lifestyle imagery, normalizes and grades all imagery to one cohesive look, produces a knocked-out device cut-out and a full-bleed outpainted cover hero, and lays out the spec tables and feature pages so every interior page is visually consistent. NOTE: because AI object/cable removal and generative fill are not available connector capabilities, the device hero is treated as an already-clean capture rather than a raw shot the connector cleans up; the legitimate connector retouch work is background knock-out and AI canvas-extend (outpaint), not object erasure.\n\nDeliverable framing: the brief's PRIMARY deliverable is the digital brochure (an ~8-page premium digital brochure) plus a print-ready PDF; the print master is therefore added as a deliberate enrichment. Constraints: A4 portrait (210x297 mm) with 3 mm bleed page geometry; a print master at 300 dpi assembled and print-prepped (RGB->CMYK soft-proof + crop-mark overlay) as a LOCAL print-prep finish, since CMYK colorspace conversion and crop-mark stamping are not headless-attested connector capabilities; a second screen-optimized digital PDF at 150 dpi, RGB, no crop marks (live hyperlink injection is out of scope - no tool injects PDF links); a single cohesive image grade (cool, high-key, low-saturation) across both client-shot and Stock-sourced imagery; brand-appropriate European-medical typography; and exactly 8 pages in the order above. Deliverables are the digital PDF and the print-ready PDF, plus a Firefly concept board (image previews only) capturing the supplied->cut-out/outpaint->graded->laid-out provenance for client sign-off.
