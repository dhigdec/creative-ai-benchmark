## One-line ask
Build Tech World PC a production-ready corporate-tech brand identity system from their rough AI concept logo: vectorize it into a clean hexagon/TW-monogram mark, render Icon-Only/Light-BG/Dark-BG/Single-Color variants at exact transparent sizes, populate user-picked Adobe Express templates (business card, eBay banner, 'Play the Latest Games' promo, Facebook cover, YouTube banner) with the brand name/taglines and export them at spec dimensions, composite a listing watermark and brand guide, animate a short preset logo sting, and assemble everything into one Firefly concept board.


## Brief
Tech World PC is a growing custom-computer company (custom gaming PCs, workstations, refurbished systems, computer repair, upgrades and components) that currently sells primarily through eBay and other online marketplaces and wants a professional, consistent brand identity that scales with the business. They have completed the concept phase and selected a logo DIRECTION — a geometric hexagon icon with an integrated 'TW' monogram, clean corporate styling, strong readability at small sizes — but the only artifact they have is rough AI-generated concept artwork from brainstorming. They are explicit that they do NOT want a blind trace of the AI image: they want the concept recovered into clean professional vector form. (Note: the connector set provides image_vectorize for raster->clean-SVG recovery but no bespoke vector path-editing tool; this spec therefore treats the cleaned vectorize output as the connector-feasible 'rebuilt vector' and notes that any further hand path-refinement is an out-of-connector source-file step, so the anti-trace requirement is graded against a clean, non-garbage vector rather than against path editing no tool can do.) The icon must function independently from the wordmark so it can live in watermarks, social profile pictures, product photos, stickers and future packaging. The brand must feel like a professional technology company in the spirit of Intel, Corsair, Lenovo, ASUS and EVGA — Professional, Modern, Trustworthy, Technical, Premium, Scalable — and must AVOID esports branding, mascots, cartoon characters, Twitch-style graphics and aggressive gaming-team logos.\n\nThe deliverable is a complete brand package. (1) LOGO SYSTEM: from the supplied concept, produce a clean vector mark and its required lockups — Icon-Only, Light-Background, Dark-Background (white mark on dark canvas), Single-Color (#0B1F3A) — exported as transparent PNGs at 512/1024/2048/4096px plus the editable vector (SVG) and an 800x800 profile crop. (Recolors are produced at render time by re-styling the master SVG fill — NOT by raster overlay — and the dark-BG lockup is a white-mark-on-dark-canvas composite, since that variant is two operations a single overlay cannot do.) (2) BRAND CORE: a concise brand-guide concept covering logo usage, color palette (a primary 'tech blue' #1F6FEB plus a dark slate #0B1F3A and a neutral #E8EDF4, in the Intel/Lenovo family — NOT neon esports color), typography recommendation, spacing/clear-space, and background-usage examples. (3) MARKETING & SOCIAL ASSETS built on Adobe Express templates the user selects from the gallery and filled with the real brand name and taglines (text-only fills), THEN explicitly exported to spec-exact PNGs: an eBay store banner (1200x270), a 'Play the Latest Games' promotional banner (1500x500), a Facebook cover (1640x624), a YouTube banner (2560x1440 with safe area), a business card (1050x600), and a profile-picture crop (800x800). A listing watermark (the Icon-Only mark over a product photo at reduced opacity) is produced by a local layout composite, NOT by generative/solid fill. (4) MOTION: a short animated logo sting (~3-4s, square 1080x1080) — the refined mark placed into a real Express/Canva design and animated with the PRESET fade/zoom reveal the connector supports (no bespoke keyframe 'assembling' build, no esports glitch). (5) PRESENTATION: every render — the refined mark, the colorway, the populated templates, the watermark demo, the brand guide and the sting — assembled into one Firefly concept board the client can review as the 'cohesive brand system' before final source-file handoff.\n\nHard constraints the agent must respect: the mark must be derived from the SUPPLIED concept (consistent hexagon + TW monogram), not invented from scratch; all logo PNG exports must be on a TRANSPARENT background at the four exact sizes (512/1024/2048/4096); the single-color and dark-background versions must be legible (no color-on-color illegibility); the brand name 'Tech World PC' and exactly the listed taglines must appear correctly spelled across every template via text-only Express fills (layout and chosen corporate font not visually broken) AND must be exported to the exact deliverable dimensions; the palette/styling must read corporate-tech with zero esports/mascot/Twitch cues; the animated sting must use the SAME refined mark; and the final concept board must contain every produced asset so a single deep-link presents the whole system. No step may use a not-available capability (generative fill, AI object/element removal, photo compositing-by-prompt, text-to-image, image->PDF, or video trim-to-timestamp).

**Iterative chaining:** The chain is genuinely output->input throughout. The client's rough concept is uploaded with full presigned-PUT plumbing (1->2->3), de-backgrounded (4), then vectorized into the single master SVG (5) that EVERY downstream logo asset descends from: document_render_vector renders the master to exact transparent sizes + profile crop (7) and to recolored single-color/white marks (8); a local composite builds the white-mark-on-dark Dark-BG lockup (9); the rendered Icon-Only mark is composited over the product photo for the watermark (18) and into the promo banner (19); the master is placed into a real design doc (21) and animated into the sting (22). The brand-brief JSON is the cross-step text spine, feeding font_recommend (11), both fill_text passes (12,13), both promo/brand-guide renders (19,20) and the animation (22), so the brand name and the literal 'Play the Latest Games' tagline are spelled identically everywhere. search_design (10) feeds both fill_text passes; the export step (14) turns the filled Express designs into spec-exact PNGs; asset_inline_preview (23) reads back all renders as a verification checkpoint; create_firefly_board (24) ingests the union of the produced system. The product photo is its own URN branch (15->16->17) consumed by the watermark and promo composites. Nothing is a dead-end; the master SVG alone fans out to six distinct downstream branches.
