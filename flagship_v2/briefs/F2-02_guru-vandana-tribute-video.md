## Brief
Context: A family is organizing a "Guru Vandana" (guru-reverence) cum birthday celebration for Shri Anil Tolia Ji, to be attended by roughly 250 friends, family members, children, and guests. The centerpiece of the function is an emotional life-journey tribute video projected on a large LED screen/projector during the event. The piece must feel emotional but positive, respectful and heartwarming, inspirational, elegant and premium — and must AVOID loud flashy editing, overly dramatic effects, fast social-media-style cutting, and distracting animations. The desired register is "cinematic family tribute / elegant slideshow-documentary," with smooth transitions, slow zoom/pan, and tasteful typography.

The ask: Using the supplied raw materials — chronological photo sets (childhood, college & friendship, career, marriage & family, recent, and Guru-Vandana moments), several raw home/event video clips, and a noisy live on-stage tribute speech recorded at rehearsal — produce a 5-8 minute Full-HD (1920×1080, MP4) tribute that follows the client's exact 8-section flow: (1) Opening Tribute, (2) Childhood Journey, (3) College & Friendship Days, (4) Career & Achievements, (5) Family Journey, (6) Guru Vandana Section, (7) Birthday Celebration, (8) Closing Message. The agent must mine the raw clips for the most emotional, usable moments, auto-cut a tight cinematic "memory sizzle" from the chosen clip segments to seed the montage, clean the noisy on-stage tribute speech into a warm, broadcast-clean narration track (or a warm/mature English VO with optional Hindi/Gujarati blessing lines), score the whole thing with soft, licensed, royalty-free piano/light-orchestral/flute music that blends under — and never overpowers — the narration, and lay elegant section titles and a closing thank-you over the cut.

Constraints & deliverables: Output 1 = the stage-screen MASTER at exactly 1920×1080 (16:9), H.264 MP4, 5-8 minutes, clean enough for large-screen projection, music bed mixed roughly 14-18 dB under the VO. Output 2 = a short (≤60s) vertical 1080×1920 (9:16) social/WhatsApp share cut derived from the master's best moments (subject-aware reframed), same audio treatment. Output 3 = the cinematic sizzle (intermediate, reused in the master, kept addressable). Output 4 = the cleaned narration WAV. Output 5 = an asset-credits / music-and-source sheet (PDF) listing every licensed Stock track and the source clips used, as the client explicitly requires "music and asset details used." All music must be royalty-free/licensed; narration must be warm, encouraging, emotional, mature and respectful; nothing flashy. The family expects one or two revision rounds, so the agent must keep the intermediate assets (cleaned VO, licensed score, the sizzle, the master) individually addressable for re-edits.
