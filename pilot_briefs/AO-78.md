# Creative Brief — Hearthstone Brew Co. Café Menu (single-page, print + digital)

**Client:** Hearthstone Brew Co. — an independent neighbourhood café (fictional, IP-clean).
**Engagement:** Freelance menu / layout design (build from scratch).
**Turnaround:** within one week of kickoff · **two rounds** of revisions included.

---

## 1. The ask
Design and build our full food & drinks menu as a **single-page A4 portrait** print piece — set all
**17 items** across four sections, integrate five finished dish photos on a warm kraft background, and
deliver **print-ready and digital** files that are on-brand and press-safe. We are handing you the brand
assets, the full menu copy, and a layout spec — **no locked InDesign template is supplied; build it in
your tool of choice** (InDesign / Illustrator / Affinity).

## 2. Deliverables
| # | Deliverable | Exact spec |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Print-ready menu PDF** | A4 portrait **210 × 297 mm**, **+3 mm bleed** (216 × 303 mm), **CMYK**, **300 DPI**, crop marks, fonts embedded or outlined, export **PDF/X-1a or PDF/X-4** |
| 2 | **Digital JPEG** | A4 portrait, **sRGB**, ~2000 px long edge, max quality — for the website, social & delivery-app listings |
| 3 | **Editable source** | Your working file (.indd / .ai / .afpub) + packaged fonts & linked images |
| 4 | **5 graded dish photos** | Warm, consistent grade, straightened, **4:5**, 300 DPI — usable standalone |
| 5 | **Vectorised wordmark** | Clean **SVG** of the Hearthstone Brew Co. wordmark |

## 3. Brand & art direction
- **Personality:** warm, cosy, neighbourhood, natural, elevated-but-unfussy — morning light, hand-made, honest.
- **Palette** (from our brand kit — colours must match):
  | Role | HEX | Approx CMYK (uncoated — soft-proof to the printer's profile) |
  |---|---|---|
  | **Hearthstone Green** (primary) | `#059E4B` | C97 M0 Y53 K38 |
  | **Cream** (reversed text / light fields) | `#F5EFDD` | C0 M2 Y10 K4 |
  | **Espresso** (body copy / rules) | `#3B2A1C` | C48 M60 Y72 K62 |
  | **Kraft paper** (background texture) | supplied/licensed warm tan | — |
- **Type — two families only:** Headings in a **warm characterful serif** (e.g. *Fraunces*); body & prices in a
  **clean humanist sans** (e.g. *Inter*). Both are commercially-licensed Google Fonts. Package/outline on export.
- **Logo:** use the **supplied wordmark** (vectorise it). **Do not recolour, redraw, or distort it.** Give it
  clear space at the top of the page.
- **Texture:** the kraft background should be **subtle** — legibility comes first (design for uncoated stock,
  where ink reads slightly muted).

## 4. Print production spec
- **Trim:** A4 portrait, 210 × 297 mm
- **Bleed:** 3 mm all sides (document 216 × 303 mm)
- **Safe margin:** keep all text ≥ 8–10 mm inside the trim
- **Colour:** CMYK throughout; use a rich black (not 4×100) for large dark solids
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI for placed images; vector for the logo and rules
- **Stock (FYI):** uncoated ~150 gsm natural/kraft — soft-proof accordingly

## 6. Photography (5 supplied raw shots — treat to one look)
The shots are raw phone captures: slightly tilted, dull/flat, cool café-window light, cluttered backgrounds.
Bring all five to **one warm, appetising, consistent grade** — straighten, warm the white balance to a cosy
morning-light feel, lift shadows, add vibrance, and throw the busy background **out of focus** so each dish
reads as hero. **Crop all five to 4:5.** Additionally, **cut out the flat-white coffee hero on transparency**
so it can sit on the kraft texture without a hard photographic edge.
Shots: flat white · buttermilk pancakes · avocado toast · butter croissant · cortado.

## 7. Layout requirements
- Single page, A4 portrait; wordmark at the top with clear space.
- Four clearly delineated sections — **section headers visibly distinct** from item names.
- Integrate the **five 4:5 photo frames** through the layout as visual highlights.
- **Typographic hierarchy:** section header › item name › description; **prices right-aligned / on a clear
  leader** so they scan instantly.
- Consistent **grid**, even margins, **generous whitespace** — cosy, not cramped.
- Natural reading flow (a diner scans top-to-bottom, section by section).
- Subtle kraft-paper background; ensure text contrast holds (cream reversed only on the green/espresso fields).
