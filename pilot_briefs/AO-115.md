# Creative Brief — Rukmini Silverworks 925 Jewelry Catalog (e-commerce batch retouch & isolation)

**Client:** Rukmini Silverworks — a boutique 925 sterling-silver house (fictional, IP-clean).
**Engagement:** Freelance product retouching / clipping (batch of 6 SKUs).
**Turnaround:** within one week of kickoff · **two rounds** of revisions included.

---

## 1. The ask
We're re-listing our collection on our own store and two marketplaces and need **clean, consistent,
catalog-grade product images** where the metalwork is the hero. You're given **six raw phone-shot
frames** of six pieces. This is a **batch clean-up, not a re-shoot**: retouch, colour-correct, and
**isolate each piece onto a pure-white background**, and deliver — **per SKU** — a transparent PNG and a
3000×3000 white-background JPEG. All six must look like **one matched set**.

## 2. Deliverables (per SKU × 6 = 12 files)
| Deliverable | Exact spec |
|---|---|
| **White-background JPEG** | **3000 × 3000 px** square, **sRGB**, background **pure #FFFFFF (hex-exact)**, **no drop shadow**, piece centered, max quality |
| **Transparent PNG** | Same graded/isolated master with the background removed to **true alpha transparency** (for our own layered layouts) |

## 3. Technical & brand constraints (hard)
- **Background:** pure **#FFFFFF** on the JPEGs (sample anywhere off the piece → 255,255,255). **No shadow, no grey sweep, no prop clutter.**
- **Square:** exactly **3000 × 3000 px**, subject centered with a consistent margin across all six.
- **Colour:** neutral — the silver must read **bright, cool-neutral metal** with **no warm/yellow or cool/blue cast**.
- **Edge fidelity:** the metal keeps its **true edge — no halo, no matte fringe, no chopped prongs**; **open filigree gaps, chain-link gaps and prong gaps must be cut through** to the background (not filled).
- **Consistency:** all six read as one catalog — same brightness, neutrality, and finish. Not six different edits.
- The pieces are the hero — do **not** reshape, warp, or re-render the metal; clean and isolate only.

## 4. The six SKUs (and what each needs)
1. **Filigree pendant** — warm cast, loose frame on a grey sweep with a prop box in shot. *Hardest isolation:* the open scrollwork has many interior gaps that must be cut to background.
2. **Jhumka drop earrings** — cool cast + underexposed (opposite problem to #1); neutralise and lift.
3. **Stacking band ring** — small piece on a visible prop stand; cleanly separate the ring from the stand.
4. **Chain bracelet** — open curb chain; the **gaps between links and the interior loop** must read as background.
5. **Oxidised cuff** — antique dark recesses; **hold the dark detail** (don't crush blacks) while the raised metal reads bright.
6. **Solitaire-look ring** — reflective stone + band; retain the specular highlights and a clean edge.

## 5. Retouching requirements (per SKU)
- **Straighten / level** the piece.
- **Neutralise white balance** and **lift exposure** so the silver is bright and cast-free.
- Gently push **clarity / contrast** so the metal reads crisp (and on the oxidised cuff, hold the recess detail).
- **Isolate the piece** (select subject → invert → fill the background pure #FFFFFF) with a clean, true edge.
- **Crop** to the identical centered 3000² square; export the white-bg JPEG **and** the transparent PNG.

## 6. What we're supplying (input assets — in `assets/`)
- 6 raw SKU photos: `sku01_filigree_pendant_raw.jpg`, `sku02_jhumka_earrings_raw.jpg`,
  `sku03_stacking_band_ring_raw.jpg`, `sku04_chain_bracelet_raw.jpg`, `sku05_oxidised_cuff_raw.jpg`,
  `sku06_solitaire_ring_raw.jpg`
- `rukmini_catalog_spec.txt` — our hand-off note pinning the exact constraints (hex, size, dual deliverable).

## 7. Constraints (hard)
- Background must be **pure #FFFFFF** (hex-exact) on the JPEGs; transparent PNGs must have real alpha.
- **Preserve all fine detail** — open gaps, prongs, clasps, thin links — nothing chopped or filled.
- **No generative editing** — no AI fill / object add-remove / re-render / upscaling / compositing. Clean and isolate the real supplied pieces only.
- Keep the metal's true form and colour — no reshaping, no invented reflections.
